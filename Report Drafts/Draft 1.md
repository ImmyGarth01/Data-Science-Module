### A) Discussion on the dataset
Within this project I focused on cleaning and analysing a dataset called "Global Commodity Prices from 2000 - 2026" by Muhammad Ibrahim Qasmi [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ibrahimqasimi/global-commodity-prices-2000-2026/data]. This was an open source dataset that I was able to find and download off of Kaggle.com which is an online platform that allows users to find and share datasets relevant for machine learning or data science projects. The reason why this particular dataset was chosen was because I felt it had a growing relevance in the current state of the global econony and as a result linked to the inflationary pressures within the UK, most recently indicated by inflated petrol prices. This personal interest has allowed me to think more critically within the assignment because I am impacted by the real world implications that the global commodity market has.
